An Iris for Education

Our flagship campaign to support educational access for underprivileged girls in Nepal

About Our Campaign

An Iris for Education is our comprehensive initiative to support educational access and literacy programs for underprivileged girls aged 5-17 in Nepal. Through fundraising, book drives, and awareness campaigns, we're making a tangible difference.

Campaign Purpose

Our mission is to improve literacy and educational opportunities for underprivileged girls through hosting schoolwide donation events. We collect school supplies, books, and educational materials from schools and partner organizations across the county.

All donated items are sent to reputable NGOs such as Room to Read and Pencils of Promise to directly support literacy programs and educational access for girls in Nepal.

Working Together

We believe in the power of collaboration. By partnering with established organizations, we maximize our impact.

School Partners

We partner with Key Club and UNICEF chapters to host events and spread awareness across schools in our county.

Community Partners

Local libraries and small businesses help us reach wider audiences by hosting presentations and displaying our posters.

NGO Partners

Room to Read and Pencils of Promise ensure that donations reach the girls who need them most in Nepal.
