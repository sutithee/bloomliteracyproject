Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

Fill out the contact form above selecting "Volunteering Opportunities" as your subject, and we'll get back to you with current opportunities.

Can my school host a presentation?

Yes! We'd love to visit your school. Contact us with your school's information and we'll coordinate a visit.

Where can I drop off book donations?

Check our Events page for upcoming book drive locations and dates.

Do you accept corporate sponsorships?

Yes! We welcome corporate partnerships. Select "Partnership/Collaboration" in the contact form to discuss opportunities.

How can I stay updated?

Follow us on social media, subscribe to our newsletter through the contact form, or check our website regularly for updates.

Can I invite you to speak at an event?

Absolutely! We're happy to speak at community events, schools, and conferences. Reach out with details about your event.
