About Our Campaign

The Bloom Literacy Project is partnering with Room to Read to raise $5,000 to support girls and women in their journey to literacy in Nepal.

Over 49% of women in Nepal are illiterate, and we believe that everybody deserves an equal opportunity to achieve the education they need to succeed.

Donation FAQ

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Bloom Literacy Project is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law.

How is my donation used?

100% of donations go directly to supporting literacy programs through our partner NGOs: Room to Read and Pencils of Promise. These funds provide books, school supplies, and tuition assistance for girls in Nepal.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! We offer monthly recurring donation options. Your continued support helps us plan long-term programs and make a sustained impact.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, you will receive an email receipt immediately after your donation for your tax records.

Can I donate in honor of someone?

Absolutely! Use the message field in the donation form to dedicate your gift, and we can send an acknowledgment if you provide their contact information.

How can I track the impact of my donation?

We provide regular updates through email newsletters and social media about our progress, events, and the impact we're making together.
